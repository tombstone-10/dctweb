About Us


Department of Creative Technologies is a sub-department of Faculty of Computing & Artificial Intelligence (FCAI) which also hosts secreteriat of National Center Cyber Security (NCCS). We promote interdisciplinary research. DCT faculty members, students and staff have successfully established an active team which works as a family in a very congenial environment. Accordingly, DCT successes and indicator are on the rise.
